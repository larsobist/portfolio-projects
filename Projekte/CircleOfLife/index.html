    special events

    autumn joy
    On average, there are more births in autumn than in the rest of the year. Counting back 270 days retroactively, one can determine that many babies were conceived during cosy season.
    holiday mood
    Over the holiday season, the Independence Day and Halloween fewer births are to be recognized, which could be explained examplary by planned births.
    day of love
    Compared to the rest of february, on Valentine's Day, there has been observed an above-average number of births.
    superstition?
    On the 13th of each month, it can be seen that there is a lower number of births than on the rest of the month's days.
